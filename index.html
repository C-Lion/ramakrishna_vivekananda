About Us

The Ramakrishna-Vivekananda Center of New York is an accredited branch of the Ramakrishna Order of India. The Center bases its teachings on the System of Vedanta, which combines both the religion and philosophy of the Hindus. The Center seeks to stimulate the growth of the individual's innate spirituality through lectures, discourses, publications, and individual guidance. To learn more, visit our About Page.
